Princess Samiat Abolanle Bada - Mayoress of Ikosi-Isheri L.C.D.A.

About - Biography

Born in December 28, 1973 at Ayepe Hospital, Odogbolu to the blessed family of late Alhaji Olatunji Raheem Bada from Ilesha town in Osun state and late Chief Mrs. Afolakemi Bada (JP) from Awosanya's family in Odogbolu town in Ogun state. She attended Moslem Primary School Odogbolu, and Odogbolu Grammar School respectively. One of the elitist schools in Ogun state


Due to change in environment she moved to Ajao estate high school Anthony village Lagos. She proceede to Ogun state university now Olabidi Onabanjo University for her diploma in marketing. She later proceed to the Lagos State University Ojo, where she bagged her BSC (Hons) in Geography and planning. She also has PGD in social works 2012/2013, and Masters in Social works 2017/2018...
